• Points of Controversy

6.8 Of the Four Elements, the Five Senses, and of Action as Visibles

Controverted Point: That each of these is visible.

PTS cs 1.1.1–9The discourse is verbatim identical with PTS CS 6.7, each of the “four elements”, “the organ of sight” alone, and “bodily action” being substituted for “space”. The opponent's rejoinders are severally as follows:

Andhaka: But do we not see earth, a stone, a mountain? water? fire blazing? trees waving in the wind? The eye, the ear, the nose, the tongue, the body? anyone advancing, retreating, looking forward, looking backward, stretching forth, retracting?
